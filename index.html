The stock audit process is necessary to reduce the avoidable investment on stocks or inventory to ensure proper balance in the process. As high levels of stock result in overstocking which may result in the poor value of cash flows and financial losses. We understand our task and check the statements during the process of examination. If we comes across with any fraud or discrepancy by the management of the entity, then we mention those points in our report, but it doesn’t mean that we perform an audit on assumption that management of the company might have committed fraud i.e. we use Professional Judgement and Skepticism accordingly.

Stock Audit Services

  • To update the starting stock details
  • To identify the discrepancy between stock records also known as computed stock and physical stock
  • To update actual physical stock as a stock record
  • To ensure proper handling of stocks
  • Identification of the slow-moving stock, deadstock, obsolete stock, and scrap
  • Accurate information on inventory value
